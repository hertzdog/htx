Our philosophy

Quality is like a wave.

Whatever work you do, if you transform what you are doing into art, you will probably discover that you have become an interesting person to others and not an object. This is because your decisions, made with Quality in mind, change you as well. Better yet: not only do they change you and the work, but they also change others, because Quality is like a wave. That Quality work you thought no one would notice is indeed noticed, and whoever sees it feels a little better: they will probably pass this feeling on to others, and in this way Quality will continue to spread.
— Robert Pirsig

We choose few projects and follow them with the utmost care. When we start a collaboration — with clients, partners or collaborators — it is usually the beginning of something lasting. We don't sell hours: we build systems that work.

Key milestones.
